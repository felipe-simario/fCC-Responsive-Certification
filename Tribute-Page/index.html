Mahatma Gandhi

Be the change that you wish to see in the world.

Here's a time line of Mahatma Gandhi life:

  • 1915 - Commencement of the Non-Cooperation Movement in India, striving for independence from British rule.
  • 1930 - Salt March: Gandhi led a 240-mile march to protest the British salt tax.
  • 1947 - Indian Independence: Gandhi's campaign for peaceful resistance led to the British withdrawal and India's independence.
  • 1948 - Gandhi's Assassination: He was assassinated by an extremist, but his legacy of nonviolent resistance endures to this day.

Gandhi was a beacon of hope and an example of how nonviolence and peaceful resistance can transform nations. He represented the tireless struggle for justice, equality, and peace.
